About Us

Maha Agro Mart is proud to actively support and collaborate with Self Help Groups (SHGs), Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), and individual farmers across Maharashtra. We recognize the crucial role these groups play in the agricultural landscape and strive to be a valuable partner in their success. Our commitment to empowering these groups and individual farmers extends beyond just providing access to high-quality agricultural products. We offer dedicated support, resources, and knowledge-sharing initiatives to help them optimize their farming practices, improve yields, and enhance overall agricultural productivity.By fostering collaborations with SHGs and FPOs, we aim to create a network of mutual support, enabling these entities to leverage our platform for their procurement needs and benefit from our expertise in sustainable agricultural practices. Through these partnerships, Maha Agro Mart contributes to the socio-economic well-being of the farming community and promotes collective growth in the agricultural sector.
At Maha Agro Mart, we understand the unique challenges faced by SHGs, FPOs, and individual farmers, and we are committed to tailoring our services to meet their specific requirements. By providing a platform that facilitates easy procurement, knowledge sharing, and support, we aim to strengthen these vital pillars of the agricultural community, contributing to a more resilient and prosperous farming ecosystem in Maharashtra.

Vision:

Empowering Maharashtra's farming community by leveraging technology and innovation for sustainable agricultural growth, ensuring prosperity and resilience in the agricultural landscape.

Mission:

To be the leading e-commerce platform, supported by The Maharashtra Agro Industries Development Corporation Ltd (MAIDC), providing seamless access to high-quality agricultural products and services to farmers across Maharashtra. We aim to simplify the procurement process, enhance productivity, and promote sustainable farming practices.

Objectives:

1. Accessibility: Ensure easy and convenient access to a diverse range of superior quality agricultural inputs for farmers across Maharashtra.
2. Empowerment: Empower farmers by providing them with essential resources, knowledge, and support to optimize yields and adopt modern agricultural practices.
3. Innovation: Continuously innovate and integrate technological advancements into the agricultural sector to enhance efficiency and productivity.
4. Sustainability: Promote and support sustainable farming methods, aiming for environmental conservation and long-term agricultural viability.
5. Community Support: Foster a supportive ecosystem that caters to the needs of the farming community, enhancing their socio-economic well-being.
